About Us

Thirty years of trusted counsel

Incorporated in 1992, Total Information Management Ltd. is a Nairobi-based consultancy specialising in management and information technology strategy. For over three decades we have partnered with governments, conglomerates, and development organisations to shape the ICT landscape across Africa.

Today, TiM brings together senior consultants with deep expertise in data strategy, AI governance, and digital transformation - combining institutional experience with the analytical rigour that modern decision-making demands.
