About Us

Thirty years of trusted counsel

Incorporated in 1992, Total Information Management Ltd. is a Nairobi-based consultancy specialising in management and information technology strategy. For over three decades we have partnered with governments, regulatory bodies, universities, and development organisations to shape the ICT landscape across Africa.

Our consultants bring deep expertise in policy design, digital transformation, and institutional strategy - grounded in a genuine understanding of the African context and an international perspective shaped by engagements across the continent and beyond.

We combine rigorous analytical frameworks with stakeholder-centred facilitation to deliver strategies that are not just well-crafted, but owned and implemented.
